Hamburger

Description

A delicious burger.

Ingredients

  • bun
  • 1/2# ground beef
  • slice colby cheese
  • ketchup, mayo, mustard

Directions

  1. Form beef into patty shape using hands or burger press
  2. On stovetop, place skillet and turn burner to medium heat
  3. Cook burger to desired done-ness
  4. Once cooked, place burger on bun. Add cheese and condiments to liking. Enjoy!
